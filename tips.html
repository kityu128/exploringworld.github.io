Essential Travel Tips & Guidelines

Smart advice to make your journeys safer, easier, and more enjoyable

Travel Smart

Document Safety

  • Make digital copies of all important documents
  • Store copies separately from originals
  • Email copies to yourself as backup
  • Know your embassy locations

Money Matters

  • Notify your bank of travel plans
  • Carry multiple payment methods
  • Keep small bills for tips and vendors
  • Research local tipping customs

Packing Smart

  • Use packing cubes for organization
  • Pack versatile, layerable clothing
  • Leave room for souvenirs
  • Carry essential medications in original bottles

Cultural Awareness

  • Research local customs before arrival
  • Learn basic phrases in local language
  • Dress appropriately for religious sites
  • Observe and follow local etiquette

Staying Connected

  • Check international plans with your carrier
  • Download offline maps and translation apps
  • Consider portable WiFi or local SIM
  • Use VPN on public networks

Food Safety

  • Watch where locals eat
  • Drink bottled or purified water
  • Avoid raw foods in questionable areas
  • Carry digestive aids just in case

Emergency Preparedness

Medical Emergency

Know the local emergency number (often 112 or 911)

Carry insurance information and blood type

Embassy Contacts

Save your country's embassy location and number

Register with STEP (Smart Traveler Enrollment Program)

Theft/Loss

Keep a separate record of credit card numbers

Know how to freeze accounts remotely

Pre-Departure Checklist

6 Weeks Before

  • Check passport validity (6+ months)
  • Apply for visas if needed
  • Research vaccination requirements
  • Book major accommodations

2 Weeks Before

  • Confirm all reservations
  • Arrange pet/plant care
  • Set up mail hold
  • Test pack your luggage

Departure Day

  • Double-check documents
  • Arrive at airport 3 hours early
  • Take photos of luggage contents
  • Charge all devices fully
