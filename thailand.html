Discover the Wonders of Thailand

From golden temples to tropical islands, bustling markets to tranquil mountains, Thailand offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Explore Destinations

Top Destinations in Thailand

Bangkok

Central Thailand

Thailand's vibrant capital where ancient temples stand alongside modern skyscrapers and bustling street markets.

Must-See Attractions

  • Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew - Stunning temple complex housing the Emerald Buddha
  • Wat Arun - Iconic "Temple of Dawn" with porcelain-covered spires
  • Chatuchak Weekend Market - World's largest weekend market with 15,000 stalls
  • Chao Phraya River - Take a dinner cruise past illuminated temples
  • Khao San Road - Famous backpacker street with vibrant nightlife

Local Tip

Visit temples early morning to avoid crowds and heat. Dress modestly (cover shoulders and knees) when visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit

November-February for coolest weather. April is hottest but includes Songkran (Thai New Year water festival). July-October is rainy season.

Getting Around

BTS Skytrain and MRT subway are efficient. Tuk-tuks are fun but negotiate price first. Grab (SE Asia's Uber) is widely available.

Chiang Mai

Northern Thailand

Cultural heart of Northern Thailand with ancient temples, lush mountains, and vibrant night markets.

Must-See Attractions

  • Wat Phra That Doi Suthep - Sacred mountaintop temple with city views
  • Old City Temples - Wat Chedi Luang, Wat Phra Singh, and more
  • Sunday Walking Street - Massive night market with handicrafts and street food
  • Elephant Nature Park - Ethical elephant sanctuary
  • Doi Inthanon National Park - Thailand's highest peak and stunning waterfalls

Cultural Experience

Take a Thai cooking class at one of Chiang Mai's renowned cooking schools like Thai Farm or Asia Scenic.

Best Time to Visit

November-February for cool weather and Yi Peng lantern festival. March-May is burning season (haze). June-October is rainy but lush.

Transport Tips

Old City is walkable. Songthaews (red trucks) are cheap shared taxis. Rent a scooter for mountain temples (if experienced).

Phuket

Andaman Coast

Thailand's largest island with stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and access to world-class diving.

Must-See Attractions

  • Patong Beach - Most famous beach with lively nightlife
  • Phi Phi Islands - Iconic limestone cliffs and turquoise waters
  • Big Buddha - 45m-tall marble statue with panoramic views
  • Phang Nga Bay - Stunning sea caves and James Bond Island
  • Old Phuket Town - Colorful Sino-Portuguese architecture

Island Hopping

Take a speedboat tour to multiple islands like Maya Bay (reopened in 2022), Bamboo Island, and Monkey Beach.

Best Time to Visit

November-April for dry season. May-October has fewer crowds but some rain. July-August sees Chinese tourists.

Getting Around

Taxis are expensive. Rent a car/scooter for flexibility. Smart buses connect main beaches. Boat tours depart from various piers.

Krabi

Andaman Coast

Dramatic limestone cliffs, pristine beaches, and access to world-famous islands like Railay and Koh Lanta.

Must-See Attractions

  • Railay Beach - Stunning peninsula accessible only by boat
  • Tiger Cave Temple - 1,237 steps to a mountaintop shrine
  • Hong Islands - Beautiful lagoon with snorkeling
  • Emerald Pool - Natural mineral pools in the jungle
  • Koh Lanta - Relaxed island with long sandy beaches

Adventure Tip

Try rock climbing on Railay's limestone cliffs - suitable for beginners to experts with many schools offering courses.

Best Time to Visit

November-April for best weather. May-October has lower prices but some rain. July-August sees European families.

Transport Tips

Longtail boats connect beaches and islands. Rent a scooter for exploring inland. Airport connects to major Thai cities.

Ayutthaya

Central Thailand

Ancient capital with magnificent temple ruins, a UNESCO World Heritage Site just north of Bangkok.

Must-See Attractions

  • Wat Mahathat - Famous for Buddha head entwined in tree roots
  • Wat Chaiwatthanaram - Impressive Khmer-style temple complex
  • Ayutthaya Historical Park - Vast area of temple ruins
  • Wat Yai Chai Mongkhon - Large reclining Buddha and chedi
  • Bang Pa-In Palace - Royal summer palace with mixed architectural styles

Unique Experience

Take a boat tour around the island to see temples from the river, just as ancient traders would have approached the city.

Best Time to Visit

November-February for cooler weather. Early morning visits avoid heat and crowds. Avoid weekends when Bangkokians visit.

Getting There

1.5-2 hours from Bangkok by train, minivan, or organized tour. Tuk-tuks and bicycles are best for exploring the ruins.

Pai

Northern Thailand

Bohemian mountain town with hot springs, waterfalls, and stunning valley views in Mae Hong Son province.

Must-See Attractions

  • Pai Canyon - Narrow ridges with spectacular sunset views
  • Sai Ngam Hot Spring - Natural thermal pools in the jungle
  • Tha Pai Bamboo Bridge - Scenic walk through rice fields
  • Mae Yen Waterfall - 2-hour hike to beautiful cascades
  • Walking Street - Night market with local crafts and food

Local Vibe

Pai has a relaxed, hippie atmosphere with many vegan cafes, reggae bars, and yoga studios. Perfect for digital nomads.

Best Time to Visit

November-February for cool, dry weather. March-May is hot. June-October is rainy but lush green with fewer tourists.

Getting There

3-4 hour minivan ride from Chiang Mai (762 curves!). Rent a motorbike to explore surrounding countryside.

Seasonal Highlights

Cool Season (November-February)

Best time to visit overall. Chiang Mai's Yi Peng lantern festival (November), Christmas in Bangkok, Chinese New Year celebrations. Ideal beach weather in south.

Hot Season (March-June)

Songkran water festival (April) nationwide. Good for northern mountains before rains. Islands less crowded before European summer holidays.

Rainy Season (July-October)

Lush green landscapes, fewer tourists. Andaman coast (Phuket) has most rain. Gulf islands (Koh Samui) better in this period. Vegetarian Festival in Phuket (October).

Regional Variations

North: Cool Nov-Feb, burning season Mar-Apr. Central: Hot Mar-May. South: West coast best Nov-Apr, east coast best Jan-Sep. Plan accordingly!
